About Me

Hello, my name is Amanda Ly. My major is English. On Fictionpress, havenseeker is my alias. I enjoy writing short stories, lyrics, & poetry, butchering perfectly decent songs, leafing through risible manga, picking up on arbitrary and mostly useless stuff. I usually weave “horror” stories, intended-to-be-funny stories, food/place descriptions, and a lot of poetry that doesn’t rhyme. But, as long as I get in character, I can attempt to write in any genre. If push comes to shove, I’ll even write romance. Articles. Screenplays. Speeches. I’m willing to create, too. If you need editing help or an extra eye to proofread your essays, I can offer assistance.
